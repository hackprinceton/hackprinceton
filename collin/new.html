Questions? Fire Away.

Who can come?

We welcome undergraduate students from all universities. Unfortunately, you must be over 18 to attend. HS Hackers, we hear you, and we're working hard on getting this changed!

How to I sign up?

Signups will be open soon on this site. A lottery will be held to distributes the available spots.

I'm allergic to so and so. Will you have food I can eat?

Yes we will! In addition to the standard victuals provided, we will make sure to have food that satisfies your dietary condition. Please let us know on the signup form.

Do I need to have a ton of experience?

0nly th3 41337 may c0m3. Just kidding!
We're open to participants of all skill levels. We have an incredible staff of mentors here to help you out if you're just getting started, or even if you've been coding since you could walk. Because even then, they're like Yoda and you're just Luke.

How can I get to HackPrinceton?

We will be sending out buses to schools with the most signups. If there is not a bus near your school, we will be offering reimbursements of up to $150 per person. All we ask is that you finish your hack!

What should I bring?

Laptop, cellphone, chargers tend to be needed at an even such as ours. We recommend non-Princeton students should also pack a change of clothes, and toiletries. Last of all don't forget to bring your friends and teammates!

What sizes can teams be?

Teams are composed of 2-5 people, of any combination of schools, age and gender. Though participants may work alone, we highly encourage you to join a team and make new friends!

What will the weekend look like?

Start Friday evening. End Sunday afternoon. Awesomeness in between.
