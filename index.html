Questions? Fire Away.

Who can come?

We welcome undergraduate and graduate students from all universities. Unfortunately, you must be over 18 to attend. HS Hackers, we're working hard on getting this changed!

How to I sign up?

Signups are closed, and lottery results are now available. If you're on the waitlist, keep an eye out; we may be able to open a few more spots! Princeton students are automatically accepted.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

We've got you covered! We will make sure to set aside meals that satisfies your allergy or dietary condition. All we ask is that you let us know when you confirm your invitation.

Do I need a ton of experience?

Not at all! We welcome students of all skill levels. We have an incredible staff of mentors here to help out if you're just getting started, or even if you've been coding since you could walk. Because even then, they're Yoda and we're just Luke.

How can I get to HackPrinceton?

We will be sending out buses out to the schools with the most signups. Destinations will be announced alongside lottery results. If there is not a bus near your school, we will be offering reimbursements of up to $150 per person. You just need to finish your hack!

What should I bring?

Laptops, cellphones, and chargers tend to be helpful at events such as HackPrinceton. We recommend non-Princeton students also pack a change of clothes, and toiletries. Just nothing dangerous please. Of course, don't forget to bring your friends and teammates!

What big can my team be?

Teams are composed of 2-5 people, involving any combination of school, age and gender. Though participants may work alone, we highly encourage you to join a group and make new friends!

What will the weekend look like?

Our opening ceremony kicks off early Friday evening, and general fun ensues until Sunday afternoon, when we bid adieu and all get some shuteye. There will be plenty of awesomeness in between.

My question wasn't frequently asked. How do I get in touch?

Ping hackprinceton[@]princetoneclub.com, and we'll get back to you as soon as we can! You may be surprised at the odd hours we'll be up.
